# Phase 03 - Multi-person Tab

**Status:** pending
**Priority:** medium
**Date:** 2026-04-02

## Overview
Manage attendance for multiple workers. Each person has their own daily wage and attendance records.

## Implementation Steps

### 1. Person management
- [ ] Add/edit/delete person (name + daily wage)
- [ ] Person list sidebar or dropdown selector

### 2. Person attendance
- [ ] Same table as personal tab but for selected person
- [ ] Switch between people to view/edit their records
- [ ] Quick-add and copy from previous day per person

### 3. Team overview
- [ ] Summary table: Person | Days worked | Total salary | Advances | Net salary
- [ ] Total salary to pay across all workers
- [ ] Ranking: who worked the most days/highest salary

### 4. Bulk actions
- [ ] Quick-add for all people at once (same date, same worksite, different coefficients)

## Success Criteria
- Can manage multiple people with different daily wages
- Each person's attendance tracked independently
- Team summary shows correct totals
- Ranking displays correctly
